Story · MVGE and the Table

Born from late-night games at Babson

MVGE started with a small group of friends at Babson who cared way too much about how the table felt. Some of us were into magic, some into poker, some into design—but we all agreed: the right cards and the right mat change the whole energy of the night.

We stopped settling for whatever was available and began prototyping our own. We tested paper stock, finishes, thickness, and mat materials with real players, in real games, in real dorm rooms and apartments.

Every deck and mat you see here is the result of that iteration. When you put MVGE on the table, you're not just placing a product—you're setting the tone for the entire session.
